Magic Eights Puzzle:
Start with a 3x3 grid containing the numbers 1-8 in a shuffled order and a free space
By clicking on one of the numbers adjacent to the free space, the number will move into the free space and leave a gap where it previously was
The puzzle is solved once the numbers are in the format:
1 2 3
4 5 6
7 8
with the free space in the bottom right corner.
